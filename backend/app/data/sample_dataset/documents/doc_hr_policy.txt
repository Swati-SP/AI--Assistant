Employee Code of Conduct:
All employees must maintain professionalism, integrity, and confidentiality at all times.

Holiday Policy:
The company observes the following holidays — New Year’s Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving Day, and Christmas Day.

Leave Policy:
Employees are entitled to 12 annual leaves and 8 casual leaves per year.
Leave requests should be submitted through the HR portal at least 2 days in advance.
