Leave Application Process:
Employees must apply for leave at least 2 days in advance using the HR portal.

Approval Workflow:
Managers review leave requests based on project schedules and team availability.
Employees receive email notifications once the leave is approved or rejected.

Sick Leave:
Medical certificates are required if the sick leave exceeds 3 consecutive days.

Casual and Annual Leave:
Employees are entitled to 8 casual leaves and 12 annual leaves each calendar year.
Unused annual leave can be carried over to the next year.
