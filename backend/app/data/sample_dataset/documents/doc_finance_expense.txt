Expense Reimbursement Policy:
Employees may claim official business expenses by submitting receipts and purpose details in the Finance portal.

Submission Timeline:
All reimbursement claims must be submitted within 30 days of the expense date.

Approval:
Managers verify and approve expense claims before forwarding them to the Finance department.

Processing:
Approved reimbursements are credited by the 10th of the following month.

Non-Reimbursable Items:
Personal entertainment, gifts, and non-business meals are not eligible for reimbursement.
