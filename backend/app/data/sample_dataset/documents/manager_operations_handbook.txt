MANAGER OPERATIONS HANDBOOK

Role of Managers:
- Responsible for team performance, hiring recommendations, conduct of appraisals, and approving leaves and expenses.

Hiring & Onboarding:
- Provide role descriptions; participate in interviews; ensure onboarding tasks completed.

Approvals:
- Approve timesheets, leaves, and expenses in a timely manner.
- Escalate disciplinary or complex HR issues to HR Business Partner.

Coaching:
- Regular 1:1s; set clear expectations and develop career growth plans.
